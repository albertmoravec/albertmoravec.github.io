The beginning

Aug 11, 2017 · 2 minute read · Comments

You’ve come to a place full of terrible code and mechanical keyboards obsession and I’m going to give you more of these for sure. My plan is using this blog to log progress of my coding experiments, custom mechanical keyboard projects and who knows, maybe even more in the future.

In the nearest future I’m going to publish progress of creating my home-made 75% keyboard.

Who am I?

My name’s Albert Moravec and I’m a programmer from the Czech republic with a lot of interests… I’ll eventually reveal them to you, but all you need to know for now is that I’m into programming, electronics and DIY.

As I recently got into this mechanical keyboards thing, I thought it might be a good idea to publish my work somewhere, so others can use it in theirs. If you want to contact me, feel free to send me a message on Discord - Albru#5814

What do I need from you?

I need your feedback because I’m really bad at deciding stuff, I want to hear your thoughts on my projects and I want to know if it’s good enough for others. As I’ll be documenting open source and community projects here, it is really important for me to know what should I do better and what do you expect from my projects.

Sorry for taking this post too serious maybe, I hope I’ll be able to find style that suits me and my posts in the near future.
